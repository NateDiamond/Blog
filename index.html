My Jewish Identity Blog

Introduction

Hello sorry souls who have somehow wandered onto this page or friends I have coerced into reading this! Post-Birthright, I am still struggling to figure out my "Jewish identity." The tour guide of our trip suggested that we write down our thoughts, and thus we can thank the brilliant Matan Chaim for the garbage that is my writing. If anything I say here offends you, I am sorry, but most of this is intended to be a stream of conscience #triggerwarning. Outside of minor proofing and perhaps minimal reordering, I just want the catharctic act of scribbling on a notepad, typing on a keyboard, and then uploading those words onto a server to which very few people will make requests. I don't have good mobile support yet, so if you can't get to a desktop to read, you should turn your phone sideways!
